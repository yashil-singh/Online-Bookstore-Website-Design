Research One - Home Page

In the making of the homepage, reference had been taken from the website called 'Donna's Running Club'. It is a website related to fitness. The idea of using a large sized background image with some information and button to navigate to a page on top of the backgroud image for the home page is taken from the site. In the page, similar things are implemented.

Research Two - Categories Section

In the making of the products page, reference had been taken from the website called 'Penguin Random House'. It is a website related to books same as the one created. The idea of the categories section has been taken as reference. In the page, the categories section is added to the top of the products page.

Research Three - Contact Us

In the making of the contact us page, reference had been taken from the website called 'Cami Ferreol'. It is a website of a designer called Camille Anne Ferreol. The design of the form to enter the user's name, email address and some message and a submit button is taken as reference from the site. In the page, the form to contact the website is implemented in a similar design/layout.

Research Four - Blog

In the making of the blogs page, reference had been taken from the website called 'Busy Bee Hazell'. It is a website related to blogs. Many blogs are posted in the website. The layout for presenting the blog is taken as reference from the site. In the page, the blog is presented in a similar layout as the reference website.

Research Five - Footer

In the making of the footer of the website, reference had been taken from the website called 'Avec Clyde'. It is a clothing website. They sell various kinds of clothes. The design of the footer is taken as reference from the site. In the page, a similar footer design is implemented.
